Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a custom design take?

+

Custom designs typically take 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to final fitting, depending on the complexity of the garment. For bridal wear, we recommend starting 6-9 months before your event.

What is your pricing structure?

+

Pricing varies based on design complexity, fabric choice, and embellishments. Custom pieces start at $800. We provide detailed quotes after your initial consultation. Ready-to-wear pieces range from $300-$1,200.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

+

Yes! We offer virtual consultations for clients who cannot visit our atelier in person. We can guide you through measurements and design choices via video call.

What fabrics do you work with?

+

We work with premium fabrics including silk, satin, chiffon, organza, velvet, wool, linen, and luxury blends. We source from the finest suppliers and can accommodate special fabric requests.
